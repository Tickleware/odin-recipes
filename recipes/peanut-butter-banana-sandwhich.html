The simple, yet delicious, peanut butter and jelly sandwhich has a brother that prefers raw fruit over the processed jelly or jam. Introducing the Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwhich. This sandwhich is also very simple to make and requires very few ingredients, making it a staple food for snacking at home or on the go!

The ingredients for this recipe is simple:

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • A whole banana
  • and...well that's it!

    • The directions for making this recipe is very simple. You have the 2 slices of bread (toasted or not) you spread the peanut butter on both sides with a butter knife and then you cut the banana into as many slices as you need to cover on slice of bread that has the peanut butter spread on it. Put the two slices together and you have yourself a delicious snack ready to be enjoyed!
