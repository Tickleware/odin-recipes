Nutella Croissant Recipe

A straightforward way to a delicious desert is to make yourself some Nutella Croissants! Nutella Croissants are a simple desert made from 3 simple ingredients! (unless you plan on making the croissants from scratch).

All you need is the following:

  • Bake Ready Croissants
  • Nutella
  • Butter (for the golden brown crispy croissants)

To make this desert, you'll need to layout the unbaked croissants, and spread nutella in the middle of them using a butterknife. Be careful not to add to much or you'll have some nutella running out of the croissants in the baking phase. Roll the croissants up and place them evenly on a baking sheet pan. Bake for 10-15 mins at 375 Degrees or until golden brown on top.

There you have it! An easy to make and easier to enjoy desert!
